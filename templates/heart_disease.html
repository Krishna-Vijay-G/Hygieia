About This Assessment

This cardiovascular risk assessment uses clinical parameters to evaluate your potential risk for heart disease. The analysis considers multiple factors including demographic information, symptoms, and clinical test results.

Risk Factors Evaluated:
  • Age and gender
  • Chest pain characteristics
  • Blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Heart rate response to exercise
  • ECG abnormalities
  • Stress test results
When to Seek Immediate Care:
  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness or fainting
  • Rapid or irregular heartbeat
  • Swelling in legs or ankles
  • Unusual fatigue
